Web Management Essentials+

Start Hosting

  • Shared website hosting

    Your website will be hosted on a shared server, alongside other websites we manage. While this generally does not impact performance, it does introduce a higher level of risk to your website management. For personal projects or sites with low traffic, a shared hosting plan is ideal.

  • Hosting/website management

    Website hosting typically involves allocating sufficient storage for site files, maintaining databases to ensure optimal data access speeds, regularly backing up assets to safeguard against data loss or security incidents, and applying necessary updates and patches to all files and assets. While we often include a few development hours for minor fixes, it’s important to note that website fixes are not usually covered under standard hosting services.

  • Domain management

    Domain name management is the process of overseeing the registration, renewal, and configuration of a domain name—your website’s unique address on the internet. Proper management ensures your domain remains active, preventing service disruptions or loss of ownership. It includes handling DNS settings, which control where your site and emails are hosted, and maintaining domain privacy and security.

    Domain name management is crucial because it impacts your site’s availability, email deliverability, and online reputation. Without proper renewal, you could lose your domain, potentially resulting in downtime or even allowing someone else to claim it. By staying on top of domain management, you protect your brand’s online presence.

  • Website edit sheets

    Website edit sheets are designed to communicate tasks that require a developer’s attention but may not involve extensive development work. These sheets can be used for tasks such as:

    • Small content or image edits
    • Re-arranging sections
    • Toggling sales or other time-based mechanics
    • Adding tracking codes or other scripts
    • Creating conversion pages from existing website templates
    • Making menu edits
    • DNS and other domain related edits

    Included on all packages, but the number provided is package specific.

  • Website major changes sheet

    If you need a major change to your website or are unsure how many development hours a particular request will require, we offer website major change sheets. These help initiate a conversation and ensure you have the opportunity to communicate your needs clearly and effectively.

    Independent from package costs, but can be negotiated when selecting your package.

  • Local and cloud-based backups

    We back up all of our hosted websites both in the cloud and on physical devices. Redundancy is essential to an effective backup strategy, and we strive to ensure that your backups are always available when needed.

  • Fixes for maintenance-related errors

    When websites go months or years without proper updates, things can break. As part of your hosting package, we address all errors and issues that arise from maintenance-related tasks.

  • Email and phone contact with a trained developer

    Developers are often difficult to reach and may leave client correspondence unanswered. We prioritize direct communication, connecting our clients with trained developers who know their websites inside and out.

  • One design rework per quarter

    Websites need consistent and strategic design updates to stay competitive in the constant battle for user attention. If your package includes design reworks, we will help you explore UI changes that better serve your users and implement any necessary design updates once per quarter.

  • Landing and marketing page creation

    Effective online marketing requires high-quality, unique landing pages to ensure smooth performance and deliver the expected campaign results. If your package includes landing and marketing page creation, we will collaborate with you to design and develop pages that complement and enhance your marketing efforts.
